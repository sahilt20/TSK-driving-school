Cricket Fielding Positions Guide

Fielding Positions

Close Catching (Red Zone)
  • Wicket Keeper - Behind stumps
  • Slips (1st-4th) - Edge catchers
  • Gully - Wide slip position
  • Silly Point/Mid-off - Very close fielders
  • Short Leg - Close leg side
Inner Ring (Yellow Zone)
  • Point - Square on off
  • Cover - Between point and mid-off
  • Mid-off/Mid-on - Straight fielders
  • Mid-wicket - Between square leg and mid-on
  • Square Leg - Square on leg
Boundary (Green Zone)
  • Third Man - Fine off side
  • Deep Point/Cover - Boundary off side
  • Long Off/On - Straight boundaries
  • Deep Mid-wicket - Leg side boundary
  • Fine Leg - Fine leg side
